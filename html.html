About LSPD / BLACK

Welcome to the official website of LSPD / BLACK, the dedicated Los Santos Police Department division operating within the vibrant MTA Morocco community. Our mission is to maintain peace, enforce the law, and ensure the safety and security of all citizens in Los Santos. We are a disciplined and professional force, committed to upholding justice with integrity and courage.

Our officers are trained to handle a wide range of situations, from routine patrols and traffic enforcement to high-stakes pursuits and tactical operations. We pride ourselves on teamwork, quick response, and a strong sense of community. Join us in making Los Santos a safer place for everyone.
